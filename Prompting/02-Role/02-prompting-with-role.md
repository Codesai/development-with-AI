# 02 - Role

## Goal

See how assigning a role to the AI changes the quality and style of the response.

## Instructions

1. Take the prompt you wrote in exercise 01.
2. Add a role at the beginning — tell the AI who it should be.
3. Run it and compare the output with what you got before.
4. Change the role to a new one
5. Run it and compare the output with what you got before.
6. Discuss the output with your pair/group.

## The Task

Same as before: ask the AI to write a user story for a login feature — but this time, start your prompt by assigning it a role.

**Example of adding a role (do not copy this, write your own):**

> You are a... [complete with whatever role you think fits]

## Reflection questions

- What role did you choose, and why?
- How did the output change compared to exercise 01?
- Did the tone, format, or level of detail change?
- Did different roles produce different results within your pair/group?
- What does this tell you about how the AI uses the role you assign?
